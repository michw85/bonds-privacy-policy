⚡ Bonds — Data Deletion Request

You can request the deletion of your Bonds account and all associated personal data at any time.

What data will be deleted

  • Your account (username, email address, password)
  • All messages you have sent
  • All media files (photos, voice messages) you have uploaded
  • Your push notification token

What data may be retained

  • Anonymized usage logs may be retained for up to 30 days for security and abuse prevention purposes

How to request deletion

Send an email to: bonds.app.dev@gmail.com

Subject: Account Deletion Request — Bonds

Include in the email:

  • Your username in the Bonds app
  • The email address you used to register

We will process your request within 7 business days and confirm deletion by email.
